The Challenge

Ozlo Sleep had a superior product but zero brand awareness in a saturated sleep market. Their previous agency produced polished content that looked great but drove no measurable sales. They needed a complete rethink of how they showed up online.

Project Image
Project Image

The Approach

We built a content-first campaign strategy anchored around real customer stories and pain points. Two shoot days produced enough raw material for six months of social content, paid ads, and email assets. Every piece was designed to convert, not just look cinematic.

Campaign Reel / BTS

4.2xPaid media ROAS

The Results

The campaign generated 9.5 million organic views across platforms within the first 90 days. Paid media ROAS hit 4.2x within the first month. Email open rates doubled with the new creative. The content system is still producing results six months later.
