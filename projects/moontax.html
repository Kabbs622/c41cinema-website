The Challenge

Moontax was launching a crypto tax platform into a crowded fintech space. They needed to build trust fast with an audience that's naturally skeptical of new financial tools. Their existing landing page converted at under 1%.

Project Image
Project Image

The Approach

We rebuilt their entire top-of-funnel: a new brand video explaining the product in 60 seconds, a redesigned landing page built for conversion, and a paid media strategy targeting crypto-native audiences on YouTube and Meta.

The new landing page converted at 3.8%, nearly 4x the original. The brand video alone generated 2.1 million impressions in the first campaign flight. Cost per acquisition dropped 62% compared to their previous agency's benchmarks.
