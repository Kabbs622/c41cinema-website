05 FAQ

Questions we
get a lot.

How much does a project cost?

It depends on scope. A single video project starts around $3,000-5,000. A full campaign with video, ads, web, and content strategy ranges from $10,000-25,000+. We always give a clear quote before work starts, and we’ll tell you honestly if your budget doesn’t match your goals.

What makes C41 different?

Most agencies do one thing well and outsource the rest. You end up managing multiple vendors. We handle video, ads, web, social, and brand under one roof. One team, one point of contact, everything built to work together.

How long does a project take?

Single video: 2-4 weeks. Website: 3-6 weeks. Full campaign with multiple deliverables: 4-8 weeks. We give a clear timeline upfront and stick to it.

Do you work outside Utah?

Yes. We’re based in Utah but work with brands everywhere. For on-location shoots, we travel. For ads, web, social, strategy, and consulting, location doesn’t matter.

Do I need to know what I need?

No. Most clients come knowing something isn’t working but not exactly what to do. The discovery call is specifically for that. We’ll identify the biggest opportunities and recommend what will move the needle.

Can I hire you for just one thing?

Absolutely. Just a video, just a website, just ad management. But the real value is when everything works together. We’ll never pressure you into more than you need.
